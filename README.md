📊 Retail Superstore Sales & Profit Analytics Dashboard
📌 Project Overview

The Retail Superstore Sales & Profit Analytics Dashboard is an interactive Power BI project designed to analyze retail business performance through key metrics and visual insights. The dashboard enables users to monitor sales, profit, orders, customer segments, and regional performance to support data-driven decision-making.

🎯 Business Problem Statement

Retail organizations generate large volumes of transactional data, making it difficult to identify profitable segments, top-performing products, and regional trends using raw data alone.

This dashboard aims to transform raw sales data into meaningful insights that help stakeholders:

Monitor overall business performance.
Identify high-performing regions and customer segments.
Analyze product contributions to revenue.
Understand sales trends over time.
Support strategic decision-making.
